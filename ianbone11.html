IAN has attempted to cross train in programming in personal time, but has lacked two key ingredients to make it happen:


  1. His current role has no requriement for coding, so anything learnt is not immediately put into practice and is quickly forgotten.
  2. Without a support network around your training, encouraging you, offering help, setting objectives, targets, challenges you quickly loose enthusiasm and direction.


The opportunity to dedicate training time at CodeClan is the stepping stone Ian needs to gain key skills and experience. Immediatly putting new skills to practice in coursework and projects, proving skills and knowledge which will seemlesly feed into conversations in interviews. Additionally, CodeClan is a safe and supportive environment, delivering support when required, guidance as needed, helping you to learn.
