IAN is a keen cyclist and a member of the Ride63 Cycling club at Lochwinnoch and recently completed a Cycle Ride Leadership course and is registered as a Cycle Leader with Cycling Scotland. Additionally, supporting this role, he was also recently certified at an Emergency First Aid at Work and Outdoors course ran by the First Aid Training Co-operative, also funded by Cycling Scotland.


He enjoys traveling abroad, spending quality time with his family on holiday and has recently visited places like Florida, Las Vegas, Vancouver, and up into Alaska. (His favourite destination is Las Vegas where he has visited about 10 times, mainly for the 5* lifestyle!). Other exciting trips have included cycling holidays in the mediteranean or city breaks.
