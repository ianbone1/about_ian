IAN currently specialises in Datacentre Finances and chargeback models in the Financial Services Industry for JP Morgan and is the location lead for the Market Data Services (MDS) team in Glasgow. The MDS team setup and manage Stock Exchange and Trading Partner Connectivity with JP Morgan around the globe and the distribution of Stock Exchange pricing data.


Ian graduated from the University of Paisley (now the University of West of Scotland) with a computing major and went on to work at IBM in the mid to late 90's, where he was a Unix and Database administrator. He moved to Clydesdale Bank for the Millenium and stayed there for 8 years, re-training in Storage Administration and fibre optic networks, leading the Storage Management Team before moving for a short period at Barclays Bank. On the 1st of March 2019, Ian will have worked at JP Morgan for 9 years in Project and Finance Management positions and currently leads a team of 17 staff.


Despite having spent a lot of his earlier career in technical roles, basic Unix Shell Scripting was the only programming Ian encountered. Since moving to JP Morgan Ian has not logged into a server or performed any techinical tasks and has become significantly de-skilled which is blocking his ability to transition into more technical roles. However, he is a wiz with Microsoft Excel and is the JP Morgan Excel training coach for Glasgow!
